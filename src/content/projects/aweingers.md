The Sono ENSTA association (which I was part of at the time) is responsible each year for organizing the party of ENSTA Paris's Integration Weekend in September. The theme for 2023 was "Avengers" (hence the name). I handled the scenography with my friend Robin, and we then set up the stage with the other members of the association.

## Preparation

Preparation began about 3 months in advance (in June 2023). After some thought, we decided to build an Iron Man head out of white wood, about 4.5 meters tall, onto which we would project colored shapes (a technique called *Projection Mapping*). Here is a screenshot of the stage preview in Blender:
![Blender screenshot](/images/aweingers-01.png)

We then had to project each of the faces onto the wooden boards in order to trace the cutting lines.
