This digital camera was inspired by the Nishika N8000. It was a very special camera built in the 80s that 
had some success recently because it is the only camera able to do true wigglegrams. 
I decided to make one but digital because the N8000 is very hard to find and was not build to last. 
I started with a Pi Zero, 4 Pi V2 Camera Modules and an Arducam multiplexer, but it way too slow. 
I am still working on a new version that will be fast enough to take true wigglegrams.
